ABOUT BIRDS

Eagle


Description
  • The eagle is a very powerful and predator bird.
  • The species of eagle is found almost all over the world, it has more than 60 species.
  • The eagle has two claws, two light feathers, two eyes, a curved pointed beak, and sharp nails in the claws.
  • The eagle flies at an altitude of about 10,000 feet.
